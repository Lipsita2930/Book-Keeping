Expense

Expenses are records of the amount a company spends on day-to-day costs during a given accounting period.

Journal

Journal is a detailed account of all the financial transactions of a business,future reconciling of accounts

Accounts

An account refers to assets, liabilities, income, expenses, and equity, as represented by individual ledger pages.

Reports

Accounting reports are compilations of financial information, derived from the accounting records of a business.
